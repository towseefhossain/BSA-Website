Our Events

We try to host multiple events per term to bring the community together. In the past, we have hosted events for Crying Hour, Henna Days and Meet and Greets. Do follow our facebook page to keep up with all the events we will be hosting in the future!

BSA Winter 2019 Meet and Greet

24 January 2019

Come and join us for our first event of the term and meet your execs for the term. We are very excited to share with you our plans for the next three months. Come and bring your friends for a night of chai, snacks and good Bengali music.

Crying Hour

28th February 2019

Are you stressed out by the monster of a schedule handed to you by the university? Do you sometimes feel like you’re drowning in your overpriced textbooks? Do you want to cry about your midterms, and life in general? Then boy, oh, boy, do we have the event for you! Come to UWaterloo BSA’s Crying Hour! We have FIFA, board games, copious amounts of desi snacks accompanied by students’ misery and tears. For only $5, you can join your fellow students in crying and complaining about life.

Spring 2019 Exec Elections

March 21st 2019

HEY HEY BSA!! It's time to pick our next term's exec members. Please take a moment to fill out this form and apply to be a part of the Spring 2019 BSA team. We look forward to hearing from you. The deadline to submit a form is Thursday, March 21st, 2019 @ 5pm.

Experience a festive evening packed with entertainment, songs, dances, stalls having mouthwatering ‘tok-jhal-misthi’ food, different traditional items like sarees, jewelry and much more! If anyone wants to perform or volunteer for the event, let us know on the Facebook page.
